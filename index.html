Australia's Largest Online Pet Shop

Pet Circle is Australia's largest online pet shop. We want to create a world where pets and their families are healthy, happy and spend more time together. That's why we stock thousands of premium and natural brands dog food and cat food brands like Royal Canin, Advance, Hills Science Diet, Black Hawk and Instinctive Bite, and offer free, fast delivery. We also stock pet food to support health concerns and special needs, including vet prescription diets such as Royal Canin Veterinary Diets and Hill's Prescription Diet, along with flea, tick and worming treatments and other medications from Nexgard, Advocate, Sentinel, Bravecto, Comfortis and Revolution.

We aim to make buying pet supplies and pet food online easy and convenient. Over 500,000 Australian pet parents shop with us because of our free shipping in metro areas, our price match guarantee, honest and expert advice from our qualified customer service team, and fast, nationwide delivery direct to your door.

We also carry a huge range of pet accessories - everything from dog beds, dog treats, dog collars, dog coats, dog toys, dog barking collars, cat litter, cat scratching posts, cat toys, to a variety of fish, reptile, bird, rabbit, and horse products!
